About me

I'm a first year student at the University of Wisconsin Madison on the Computer Science Track.

Background

I went on to amass almost 20+ offers from the D3/NAIA level to the Divison One level granted, they had a large roster. However, I put the student in student-athlete; I used the same drive and determination I used on the field to achieve far more extraordinary feats in the classroom. As of right now, I'm working on projects and honing my craft, including work far beyond the classroom. I also work in IT Tech Support, and I'm involved in research at my University.

I do what I love and love what I do. Don't forget #AGTG!!
