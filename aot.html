Our Mission

When I played soccer in high school, I felt lost in the recruiting process despite having amassed 20+ offers from the D3/NAIA level to D1. At the time, I had wished that there was a guiding hand and a way for me to get more exposure. I ultimately stepped away from the game; however, I made it my life's work to make sure no more untapped gems of potential go under the radar. So I founded Athletes of Tomorrow, a recruiting networking site that I use to match unpolished gems with top clubs or universities. Our alum includes 20 professional athletes, 50+ division one athletes, and 300+ from D2 down to D3/NAIA and JUCO.
